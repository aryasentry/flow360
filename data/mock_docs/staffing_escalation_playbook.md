# Strategic Staffing Escalation Playbook

If a strategic account has renewal within 90 days and an SLA breach risk, create an executive check-in within 48 hours.

For critical healthcare roles starting within 5 days, credentialing blockers must be escalated to the compliance lead before candidate presentation.

If premium rates exceed 10 percent above card, include a cost-risk explanation and replacement guarantee options.

For accounts marked amber or red, every next best action must name an owner and due date.
