# Policy: Field Safety Verification Before Dispatch

Document owner: Safety Compliance Office  
Policy owner: Ishita Rao  
Applies to: PrithviGrid field maintenance and outage response work  
Version: 2026.06  
Classification: Company policy, safety compliance

## Purpose

This policy defines the minimum safety checks required before a technician or crew can be dispatched to field work involving transformers, hospital feeders, municipal feeders, or high-voltage equipment.

## Mandatory Checks

Before dispatch, the operations team must confirm:

- current technician safety refresher certificate
- lockout-tagout checklist readiness
- PPE verification
- site permit status
- weather risk review
- asset criticality
- customer ETA communication requirement
- backup crew option for critical feeders

## Blocking Conditions

Dispatch is blocked when:

1. Refresher certificate is expired.
2. PPE verification is missing.
3. Lockout-tagout checklist is incomplete.
4. Site permit is not available.
5. Weather risk makes field work unsafe.

The operations team may not override these conditions without written safety approval from Ishita Rao or the designated safety approver.

## Customer Communication Rule

If safety approval is pending, customer communication must say "safety approval pending" rather than implying that crew movement has started. For hospital feeders, the customer update must include next update time and backup crew status.

## Flow360 Memory Rule

Future recommendations must treat safety status as a first-class decision input, not as an afterthought. If previous SLA breach or near-miss memory is retrieved, recommendations should explicitly mention how the action prevents repeat failure.
