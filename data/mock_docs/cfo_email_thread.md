## Thread Interpretation For Flow360

This email thread establishes a business rule: premium-rate candidates cannot be treated as approved unless the approval brief is prepared and the candidate's credentialing status is clear. The next best action should combine commercial approval and credentialing readiness. If a candidate has pending license verification, the system should not recommend direct CFO approval as the only step; it should first recommend resolving or labeling the credentialing blocker.
