## Compliance Rules Restated

Clinical candidate presentation must follow this sequence:

1. Confirm active license verification.
2. Confirm BGV completion.
3. Confirm immunization and facility packet.
4. Confirm start date and shift availability.
5. Check rate variance and replacement guarantee eligibility.
6. Mark candidate as ready, conditional, or backup.

No replacement guarantee should be attached to a candidate who is not fully verified. The guarantee creates commercial exposure if the candidate drops or is rejected by compliance.

## Decisions

The team agreed that the shortlist should not hide blockers. Ananya Sharma and Riya Menon may remain in the shortlist, but only under conditional status. Devika Iyer should be treated as the safest ready option if night-shift confirmation is captured. The recruiter must provide two backup names where verification is already complete, even if fit score is lower.
