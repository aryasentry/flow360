## 1. Account Overview

Aarogya Health Network is a strategic healthcare staffing account with 42 active facilities across Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad, Mysuru, Nashik, and nearby tier-two clinical clusters. The account purchases urgent clinical staffing, emergency coverage support, and selected health-IT staffing services for Epic implementation work.

The account is commercially important because annual renewal exposure is estimated at INR 15.2 Cr and the customer has historically expanded volume when service reliability is strong. The account is operationally sensitive because staffing delays affect ICU coverage, emergency ward coverage, overtime usage, and facility administrator confidence.

## 6. Commercial Context

Rohan Kulkarni is willing to approve premium rates when the account team quantifies why premium is better than unfilled shifts. He does not want vague urgency claims. Commercial approval should include:

- Role and facility.
- Candidate fit and credentialing readiness.
- Requested rate variance.
- Vacancy cost.
- Overtime exposure.
- Backup candidate status.
- Recommendation from Flow360 account manager.

Premium approvals above 12 percent require CFO review before client-facing commitment.

## 7. Service Commitments

Aarogya expects urgent staffing support to meet the following operating standards:

- Critical shortlist within 48 hours.
- Credentialing status visible before candidate presentation.
- Same-day escalation for blockers inside five days of start.
- Daily update during critical hiring cycle.
- Replacement plan when a selected candidate drops before day 10, subject to credentialing terms.
