# Aarogya Health Network - Competitor Intelligence And Renewal Defense Brief

Document ID: AHN-COMP-INTEL-2026-Q3
Prepared By: Flow360 Account Strategy
Last Updated: 2026-06-27
Classification: Internal renewal planning document

## 1. Executive Summary

A competing staffing vendor has entered the Aarogya renewal conversation with a simple commercial message: lower premium cap and a broad replacement guarantee. The competitor's offer is attractive at first glance because it is easy for procurement to compare. However, the competitor's known weakness is slower specialty coverage, particularly Epic analyst staffing and structured compliance visibility.

Flow360 should not respond only with price matching. The better renewal defense is to demonstrate operational reliability: verified shortlists, transparent credentialing status, named escalation ownership, and CFO-ready cost-risk explanations.

## 2. Competitor Offer Signals

| Competitor Claim | Customer Appeal | Flow360 Counter Position |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flat 6 percent premium cap | Easier budgeting | Show when premium protects coverage and avoids overtime |
| 10-day replacement guarantee | Perceived risk reduction | Offer guarantee only for fully credentialed candidates with clear exclusions |
| Faster sales response | Executive comfort | Provide named escalation owner and daily updates |
| Lower blended staffing cost | Procurement leverage | Compare total cost of vacancy, overtime, and delayed starts |
| Broad nursing pool | Coverage confidence | Show verified/conditional/backup labels and actual availability |

## 3. Flow360 Strengths

Flow360 can differentiate with decision quality, not just staffing volume:

- Transparent compliance tracking.
- Explicit verified, conditional, blocked, and backup labels.
- Faster shortlist delivery when evidence is complete.
- Named escalation ownership.
- Premium approval briefs for CFO review.
- Memory of prior incidents and corrective actions.
- Ability to connect CRM, meeting notes, policy, candidate status, and renewal risk.

## 4. Renewal Defense Strategy

The renewal defense should focus on the risk of hidden blockers. A lower premium cap is not useful if the candidate cannot start. A replacement guarantee is not valuable if exclusions are unclear. Flow360 should show that its platform reduces execution risk by making the next best action explainable and evidence-backed.

Recommended proof points:

1. May SLA RCA corrective actions completed.
2. Current shortlist labeled by readiness status.
3. Premium-rate approval brief prepared before CFO review.
4. Replacement guarantee wording tied to verified credentialing.
5. Flow360 memory shows previous mistake and prevents recurrence.

## 5. Recommended Next Best Action

If competitor pricing is raised during renewal discussion, Flow360 should recommend:

"Acknowledge the pricing concern, then send a renewal defense note showing how verified credentialing, backup candidate visibility, and premium-rate justification reduce total operational risk. Include May RCA corrective actions and the current segmented shortlist."

This action is stronger than discounting immediately because it reframes the conversation from unit price to avoided operational failure.
