# Flow360 Healthcare Staffing Replacement Guarantee Policy

Document ID: FLOW-GUARANTEE-HEALTHCARE-2026
Version: 1.5
Owner: Flow360 Legal And Staffing Operations
Applies To: Healthcare staffing accounts with approved service terms

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines when Flow360 may offer replacement coverage if a candidate drops out, fails to report, or becomes unavailable during the early service window. It is designed to protect the customer while preventing the account team from making promises that cannot be supported by credentialing readiness or available backup pipeline.

## 2. Standard Guarantee Window

The standard replacement guarantee window for approved healthcare staffing placements is 10 calendar days from the confirmed start date. The guarantee applies only when the candidate was fully credentialed before start, the client accepted the candidate, and the service agreement includes replacement terms.

## 3. Eligibility Conditions

A placement is eligible for replacement guarantee only when all of the following conditions are true:

- Candidate license verification was complete before start.
- Background verification was complete before start.
- Facility packet was complete.
- Shift consent was documented.
- Client selected the candidate after reviewing accurate status labels.
- Candidate did not fail due to client-side schedule change or facility access delay.
- Flow360 had a reasonable opportunity to provide backup coverage.

## 4. Exclusions

The guarantee does not apply automatically when:

- Candidate was presented as conditional.
- Client accepted a known credentialing blocker.
- Candidate rate was approved as an exception without backup availability.
- Facility changed role requirements after selection.
- Client delayed approval or onboarding actions.
- Candidate failed a compliance requirement that was disclosed before selection.

## 5. Approval Requirement

Before offering the guarantee to Aarogya Health Network, the account manager must confirm candidate status with compliance and verify backup candidate availability. For renewal-sensitive accounts, guarantee language must be precise. The account team should avoid broad promises such as "we guarantee replacement for any drop." Recommended wording:

"Flow360 can provide replacement coverage under the 10-day guarantee for fully credentialed candidates. Candidates presented as conditional will have replacement options listed separately, but guarantee terms depend on the outstanding credentialing item."

## 6. Flow360 Recommendation Guidance

If the customer asks for a replacement guarantee while a preferred candidate has pending license or BGV status, Flow360 should recommend a conditional response. The recommendation should identify:

- Which candidates are guarantee-eligible.
- Which candidates are conditional.
- Which backup candidates exist.
- What compliance evidence is missing.
- Who must approve the wording.

The agent should never recommend offering blanket guarantee language when credentialing is incomplete.
